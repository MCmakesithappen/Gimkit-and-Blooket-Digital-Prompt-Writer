ℹ️ About This Tool
🎯

NC Standards Aligned

Every quiz is built around the NC Standard Course of Study for your selected grade and content area.

📊

Mixed Difficulty Levels

Questions are automatically spread across ~40% Recall (L1), ~40% Application (L2), and ~20% Analysis (L3).

🎲

Randomized Answers

Answer positions are shuffled on every question so the correct answer never always appears in the same spot.

📥

Instant CSV Download

Your properly formatted .csv file downloads automatically — ready to bulk upload to Gimkit or Blooket.
